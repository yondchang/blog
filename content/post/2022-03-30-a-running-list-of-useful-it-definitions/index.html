  • AG: Availability Group
  • HA: High Availability(in percentage), \(HA=1-\frac{DownTime}{DownTime+OperationTime}\), often denoted by number of 9s in decimal place, i.e. 99.99% = 0.01%36524*60 = 52.56 minutes of downtime per year.
  • DR: Data Recovery, often refers to recovery across different physical locations.
  • RTO: Recovery Time Objective, i.e. time taken from start of recovery to completion, ideally 0 or instant recovery.
  • RPO: Recovery Point Objective, i.e. until which day the data is recovered, ideally 0 or immediate recovered to latest data
